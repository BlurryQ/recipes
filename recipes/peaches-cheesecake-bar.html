Peaches & Cream Cheesecake Bars

Ingredients

    For the Base

  • 200g light digestive biscuit (crushed)
  • 55g buttery baking spread (melted)

    • For the Filling

  • 450g cream cheese
  • 397g condensed milk
  • 280g 5% fat Greek yoghurt
  • 40ml lemon juice
  • 1 tsp vanilla

    • For the Topping

  • 400g tinned peaches (drained)

Method

  1. Step 1

    2. Put the crushed biscuits into a bowl with the melted butter. Mix it together thoroughly, then press the mixture into the base of your tin. Chill for ten minutes.

  2. Step 2

    3. Pour the condensed milk and vanilla into a large bowl, add the cream cheese and mix together with an electric hand held mixer on a slow speed, until smooth. Fold through the Greek yoghurt.

  3. Step 3

    4. Once the mix is smooth, add in the lemon juice and gently mix in until the mixture starts to thicken â€“ be careful not to over mix as it will loosen and not set.

  4. Step 4

    5. Pour the mix over the base and pop back into the fridge to chill for 2 hours (but preferably overnight!)
